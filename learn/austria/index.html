#codeEU events 2013 in Austria

  • Drupalcamp Vienna November 22-24 @ Vienna
    Learn about Drupal from the experts and attend our pre-conference trainings.
  • Vienna Wordpress Meetup November 28 @ Vienna
    We are group of passionate users, developers, geeks and artist. We use WordPress on a regular basis and some of us are really into the code (which is poetry!).We want to meet up every month and help each other, growing a solid community around our favorite platform.
  • EYA - European Youth Award workshop November 29 @ Graz
    Coding websites: skills within 60 minutes by Kerstin Kollmann
    When people talk about "the internet" today, they mostly mean the World Wide Web, a huge network of (interlinked) websites. What all websites have in common, regardless of their purpose, is that they are documents based on the same language. This workshop's objective is to offer you a look "behind the scenes" of the WWW and help you get creative yourself by teaching you the basics of building websites.
    Target audience: Beginners to HTML and CSS.
    Prerequisites: None. Please bring your own laptop if possible.
  • EYA - European Youth Award workshop November 30 @ Graz
    Code Literacy - getting rid of the "1337" in 1337 speak by Kerstin Kollmann
    This session will introduce you to recent initiatives that have made it their goal to get more people interested in IT as well as cool online resources for learning programming. During the second part of the session, we will try our own hands at practical examples demonstrating why knowing how to code can make everyday life easier (and also more fun!).
    Target audience: Beginners to programming.
    Prerequisites: None, though a basic knowledge of HTML and CSS will be of advantage. Please bring your own laptop if possible. Recommended browsers: Firefox or Chrome (either of which can be downloaded online for free).
  • Ruby on Rails Beginners Workshop November 30, 10 AM to 4 PM @ sektor5, Vienna
    During the workshop you will learn the basics of Ruby on Rails, supported by our coaches.
