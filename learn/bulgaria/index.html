Other coding initiatives

  • Telerik initiatives:
    • Telerik Academy is a free tech-ed initiative by Telerik, providing one-year cutting-edge software engineering training to young people, who want to jump start their career in the ICT industry. In 2012-2013 alone 12,050 people were enrolled in 35+ courses in 14 major areas. 2,050 of the participants attended onsite.
    • Telerik Kids Academy is children’s education initiative, teaching youngsters, aged 9-12, how to code. Over 700 children in 26 schools in 19 towns in Bulgaria currently attend the Kids Academy. Classes in Sofia are held every Sunday.
    • Telerik School Academy is a specialized yearly training for high-school students, eager to develop web and mobile applications. The Academy takes places during three days every month in Sofia, BG.
    • Telerik Algo Academy is Programming training for students of all ages who want to prepare for the National Olympiad in Informatics in Bulgaria. Two-day trainings take place every month in Sofia, BG.
