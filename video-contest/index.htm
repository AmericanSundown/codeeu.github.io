Europe Code Week ambassadors encourage all the event organizers to tell their CodeWeek stories by making short videos and sharing them on YouTube.

The CodeWeekEU-2015 Video Contest provides an additional motivation to share and promote the videos in order to keed raising awareness of the importance of coding and computational thinking beyond Code Week.

You can take part in the competition with three kinds of videos: videos made during a CodeWeek event, videos reporting the effects of the "Ode to code" tune, videos showing the results of a remix of the CodeWeekEU tutorial on Scratch. The "Ode to code" is an open source music made to celebrate Europe Code Week. It can be freely added as a sound track to your video, or remixed to make your own music. All the resources you need for your project are available on http://codeweek.it/ode-to-code/

How to participate

To take part in the video contest:

  1. Record your video
  2. Upload your video to YouTube (using your own channel)
  3. Fill in and submit the online form providing the short URL of your video and the country and category it belongs to
    . In case your video refers to a CodeWeek event or to a Scratch project, provide also the URL either of the event or of the project.
  4. Update the description of your video following the instructions in the confirmation e-mail
  5. Keep on having fun with coding, music, and dance!

Awards

The contest will end on October 31, 2015. The most popular videos (those with most views, likes, and comments on YouTube by the end of the contest) will be displayed on Europe Code Week website.

The most popular videos in each category and country will be awarded with official certificates.

Additional prizes can be offered by sponsors.
