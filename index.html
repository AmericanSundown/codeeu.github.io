P.S.: Let's keep Europe coding!

Coding is fun. Creative. Full of great people and passionate communities. You can help any time of the year. Organize a programming workshop, the one you planned to do in any case. Find a few friends who're curious about what you do and teach them how to code. Start a local study or support group for coding beginners. And remember, two is a group already.
