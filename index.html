300 #codeEU events!

The 1st Europe Code Week during November 25 - 30, 2013 was a successful experiment.

In just a month, we connected thousands of individuals, groups, organizations, and companies, who are helping more people experience the joy of coding all over Europe.

Check out the Coding Map of Europe to see over 300 events that took place during Code Week. Find other ongoing learning opportunities nearby or start planning your own.
